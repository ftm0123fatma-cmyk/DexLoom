## 📖 What is DexLoom?

DexLoom is a simple app made to run Android APK files on iOS devices that have restrictions. It is still in progress but can help you try out Android apps on jailed iOS systems.

You don’t need special skills or coding knowledge to use it. This guide will walk you through the process of downloading and running DexLoom on a Windows computer and then getting started with your iOS device.

---

## 🖥️ System Requirements

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A Windows PC running Windows 10 or later.
- At least 4 GB of free disk space.
- Internet connection to download files.
- An iOS device with iOS 13 or later.
- USB cable to connect your iOS device to your PC.
- Installed iTunes on your Windows computer (to allow iOS device detection).

## ⚙️ Common Questions

### Can DexLoom run every APK?

No. DexLoom works best with simple apps. Apps that require special hardware or Google Play services might not run correctly.

### Do I need to jailbreak my device?

No. DexLoom is designed to run without jailbreaking iOS.

### Is DexLoom safe?

Yes, DexLoom does not modify your device permanently. It runs APKs inside a controlled environment.

### What if the app crashes?

Try closing DexLoom and your connected iOS device, then start again. If problems persist, check the GitHub page for updates or support.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting Tips

- Make sure your iOS device is connected and trusted.
- Verify that iTunes is installed and detects your iPhone or iPad.
- Close other apps on your PC that might use the device.
- Use APK files from trusted sources.
- Restart DexLoom if it freezes or crashes.
- Reboot your iOS device if the connection is lost.
